# VISION

## Product Vision
Make every developer 10x more productive by automating the full software delivery loop.

## Roadmap
- **Phase 1 (Current):** BA to PR workflow with human approval and audit logs.
- **Phase 2 (6 months):** Autonomous monitoring and self-healing loops tied to production telemetry.
- **Phase 3 (12 months):** Predictive development where agents forecast likely regressions and propose preventative changes.
- **Phase 4 (18 months):** Full autonomous software company operations with portfolio-level planning and controlled execution.
